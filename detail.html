INTRODUCTION

The private cabin at our Forest Study Room provides a completely secluded and distraction - free learning environment. Each cabin is thoughtfully designed with your comfort and productivity in mind. With comfortable seating, a spacious desk, and optimal lighting, you can focus on your studies without any interruptions.

We also offer high - speed Wi - Fi and multiple charging ports to keep your devices powered up throughout your study session. The superior soundproofing ensures that you can enjoy a quiet and peaceful learning experience.

Facilities

  • Ergonomic chairs and a large, sturdy desk
  • Energy - efficient LED lighting
  • High - speed fiber - optic Wi - Fi
  • Multiple USB and power outlets
  • Soundproof walls and acoustic ceiling tiles

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own food and drinks? +

Yes, you can bring non - messy snacks and sealed drinks. However, please keep the cabin clean.

Is there a security deposit? +

Yes, a $50 security deposit is required for monthly rentals. It will be refunded upon the successful completion of your rental period.
